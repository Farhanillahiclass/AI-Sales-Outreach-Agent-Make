## 🚀 Key Features

* 📩 **Automated Lead Ingestion:** Instantly triggers whenever a new unread sales inquiry hits your Gmail inbox.
* 🔍 **Autonomous Web Research:** Extracts the sender's company domain and triggers a Make AI Web Search to pull real-time intelligence (company size, sector, pain points).
* 📊 **Smart Qualification Framework:** Analyzes the data to score leads as **HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW** priority based on budget and intent signals.
* 📅 **Google Calendar Integration:** Dynamically fetches availability for the next 7 days and inserts 2-3 specific meeting time proposals into the response for high-value leads.
* ✍️ **Ghostwritten HTML Drafts:** Writes a tailored response using professional inline-styled HTML and drops it right into your Gmail Drafts folder for a final review.
* 💬 **Slack Team Sync:** Sends an instant notification to your team's Slack channel as soon as a lead is successfully processed.

## 📦 Deployment Guide

1. Click my **[Shared Template Link](https://eu1.make.com/public/shared-scenario/Yc4hBGX2pB4/sales-outreach-agent)** to clone the blueprint into your Make.com workspace.
2. Fix the **5 core connections** that reset during cloning for security:
    * **Gmail Modules:** Connect your active workspace or business email.
    * **Make AI Agent Module:** Authorize your AI provider, choose your model, and re-map the input fields if prompted.
    * **Google Calendar & Slack Modules:** Authenticate your respective team workspaces.
3. Toggle the scheduling switch at the bottom left to **ON** to run it live.
